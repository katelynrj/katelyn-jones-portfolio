# HR Employee Attrition Dashboard

## Business Objective
Analyze employee turnover patterns and identify factors associated with employee attrition.

## Tools Used
- Microsoft Excel
- Pivot Tables
- Pivot Charts
- Dashboard Design

## Key Findings
- Overall attrition rate was 16.1%.
- Research & Development experienced the highest number of employee departures.
- Employees working overtime showed higher attrition.
- Most departures occurred within the first five years of employment.
- Laboratory Technicians and Sales Executives experienced the highest attrition counts.
